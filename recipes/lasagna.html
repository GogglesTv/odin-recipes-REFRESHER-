Lasagna

Garfield's favorite dish and one of mines as well. This recipe is sure to make any dinner a night to remember! This recipe makes 12 serving! Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • One Sweet Onion
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 28oz Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 15oz Tomato Sauce
  • 2 8oz Tomato Paste
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese:
    • Parmesean
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta Cheese
  • Seasonings:
    • Parsley
    • Bay Leaves
    • Sea Salt
    • Italian Seasoning
    • Fennel Seeds
    • Black Pepper
  • 1 Egg
